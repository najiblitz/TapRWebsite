What does a tap'r product do?

Tap'r products are designed to help with digital marketing and networking needs using NFC & QR Code technologies. We provide business cards for staying in touch with clients as well as promotional material such as posters, keychains, coasters, menus and more.

What is NFC technology?

NFC stands for near-field communication, and it allows phones, tablets, and other devices to share data with other NFC-equipped devices easily. By using this technology, you can easily share data, websites and contact information with someone instantly.
